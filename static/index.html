Ask timeless human questions in plain words — death, love, fear, meaning, ambition.

Why not just ask ChatGPT?

  • It never writes a word. Every answer is a real passage you can open to the exact chapter — nothing invented, nothing misattributed.
  • The weight is in the source. Aurelius, Dostoevsky, Seneca — minds that lived it and outlived us, not one flattened helpful-assistant voice.
  • A discovery engine, not an answer machine. Find something you didn't know existed, and a book to read next.
  • It confronts instead of coddles. The canon was written to challenge you, not to please you.
